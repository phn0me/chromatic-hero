# Crafting & Loot System

## Overview
The endgame is driven by two parallel systems: **Loot (Hunter)** and **Crafting (Artificer)**. Both are viable paths to power.

## The Grind: Randomized Dungeons
Endgame content consists of procedural maps with random modifiers that dictate enemy behavior and drop tables.

### Common Modifiers
- **Fire Storm:** All enemies are Fire-aligned. Drop: *Red Runes*.
- **Triple Threat:** Enemies have mixed affinities (e.g., Red+Yellow+Blue). They shoot complex beams. Drop: *Rare Mixed Shards*.
- **Immunity Zone:** Half the map contains enemies immune to physical damage. Forces Warrior to switch to elemental mode.
- **Mana Drain:** Wizard mana does not regenerate naturally.

## Loot Path: The Hunter
Defeat bosses and elites to find **Named Unique Items**.
- **Source:** Boss drops only (e.g., *Void Stalker* drops *"Void's Bane"*).
- **Pros:** Guaranteed high-tier stats.
- **Cons:** Fixed affixes. You might get a great sword, but with the wrong damage type for your current dungeon.

## Crafting Path: The Artificer
Farm materials in dungeons to build your own perfect gear.

### Step 1: Base Items
Found on regular mobs.
- Examples: *Iron Sword*, *Steel Shield*, *Crystal Staff*.
- Stats are random but low-tier.

### Step 2: Socketing & Gems
Use gathered resources to socket gems into bases.
- **Pure Gems:** Add specific elemental damage (e.g., Red Gem = +Fire Dmg).
- **Mixed Gems:** Add multiple elements (e.g., Orange Gem = +Fire & Lightning).
- **Strategy:** If entering a "Ice Storm" dungeon, craft a sword with Yellow (Lightning) gems.

### Step 3: Reforging
Spend gold and shards to re-roll unwanted stats on a base item.
- *Goal:* Turn a generic "Heavy Blade" into a "+Crit / +Fire Dmg" weapon specifically for a Fire-immune boss.

## Economy Balance
- **Loot** provides variety and "flavor" items.
- **Crafting** allows for optimization and adaptation to random dungeon modifiers.
- You do not need to rely solely on luck. If you find a bad drop, you can craft something better using the materials from the same run.
